\# Employee Attrition Analysis



\## Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, visualization, and business recommendations.



\## Project Files

\- `Coding\_Employee\_Attrition.ipynb` – Data cleaning, EDA, and analysis.

\- `employee\_attrition\_course.csv` – Dataset.

\- `Employee Attrition Analysis.pptx` – Project presentation.

\- `Final Business Report.pdf` – Final business report.



\## Tools \& Libraries

\- Python

\- Pandas

\- NumPy

\- Matplotlib

\- Seaborn



\## Key Insights

\- Overtime significantly increases attrition.

\- Employees with short tenure are more likely to leave.

\- Income, job role, and work-life balance impact retention.

\- Business recommendations were provided to reduce employee turnover.
